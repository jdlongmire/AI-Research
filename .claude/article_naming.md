# Article Naming Convention

## Format

```
YYYYMMDD_NN_vX_Title.md
```

## Components

| Component | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| YYYYMMDD | Date (year, month, day) | 20251202 |
| NN | Article number for that day (01, 02, etc.) | 01 |
| vX | Version or revision number (v1, v2, etc.) | v1 |
| Title | Meaningful title (underscores for spaces) | What_can_AI_do |

## Examples

- `20251202_01_v1_What_can_AI_do.md` - First article on Dec 2, 2025, version 1
- `20251202_02_v1_Scaling_limits.md` - Second article on Dec 2, 2025, version 1
- `20251202_01_v2_What_can_AI_do.md` - Revision of first article

## Notes

- Increment article number (NN) for each new article on the same day
- Increment version (vX) for revisions of the same article
- Keep titles concise but meaningful
- Use underscores instead of spaces in titles
